## The service, in plain words

Cloudoodle is a personal daily-Polaroid app. Once a day you
photograph the sky; the app's AI identifies a shape in the clouds,
develops the photo into a Polaroid stamped with the date and
weather, and saves it to a journal on your device. You can write
a short note about the day on the back of each Polaroid.

The service is provided "as is." We try to keep it running,
accurate, and pleasant, but we don't guarantee continuous
availability, perfect shape identification, or always-correct
weather.

## Free tier and Cloudoodle Unlimited

The app is free to use. The free tier includes one developed
Polaroid per day; the local journal, the daily reminder, and
everything else is unlimited.

**Cloudoodle Unlimited** is an auto-renewing subscription managed
by Apple through the App Store:

- **Pricing**: shown in the in-app Cloudoodle Unlimited screen in
  your local currency. Apple sets and displays the price.
- **Renewal**: subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each
  period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
- **Cancellation**: anytime in iOS Settings → your Apple ID →
  Subscriptions. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the
  current period; we don't issue partial refunds.
- **Refunds**: handled by Apple, not by Cloudoodle. Refund requests
  go through reportaproblem.apple.com.

If your subscription ends, your existing Polaroids and notes stay
on your device. New scans return to one-per-day.

## AI output

The shape names and watchability scores associated with each
Polaroid are generated by AI (Google Gemini, for both the
analysis and the developed image). They're playful
interpretations, not factual claims. If a generated phrase is
inappropriate, please email us — we tune the prompts as needed.

## Weather information

Forecasts come from Apple WeatherKit. They are estimates and
should not be relied on for safety-critical decisions (flying,
sailing, storm-tracking). Cloudoodle is not a weather emergency
service.

## Notifications

location permission, and you can disable it anytime in Settings.
